## Publishing Governor

Before any output becomes a feed post, it should receive a placement decision.

| Candidate condition | Placement |
|---|---|
| Strong brand fit, strong visual hook, and clear CTA | Reel or flagship carousel. |
| Strong visual but weak CTA | Rewrite caption before publishing. |
| Good challenge participation but off-brand | Story or low-priority carousel. |
| Ordinary scene with no impossible mechanic | Do not publish to feed; use Stories or skip. |
| Multiple strong outputs on same day | Publish the strongest one; hold others for later. |
