# My Custom Air Fryer Breakfast Sandwich Guide

A reliable, step-by-step guide to making a perfect breakfast sandwich using frozen sausage patties, eggs, and bread in the air fryer using a silicone pan and a ceramic chafing dish.

---

## Equipment & Ingredients
* **Ingredients:** Bread, Frozen Sausage Patties, Eggs, Seasonings (Salt, Pepper, splash of milk/water), Butter or Oil (for greasing).
* **Tools:** Air Fryer, Silicone Pan (for sausages), Ceramic Chafing Dish (for scrambled eggs).

---

## The Customized Cooking Order

### Step 1: The Sausages
* **Settings:** 370°F for 8 to 10 minutes
* **Action:** Put your frozen sausage patties into your silicone pan and air fry them. Flip them halfway through the cooking time. 
* **Outcome:** They should be fully cooked, browned, and sizzling. Pull the silicone pan out and set the sausages aside on a plate.

### Step 2: The Eggs (Scrambled)
* **Settings:** 370°F for 4 to 6 minutes
* **Action:** Whisk your eggs in a mug with a splash of milk or water, salt, and pepper. Grease your ceramic chafing dish really well, pour the eggs in, and place the dish into the air fryer.
* **Pro-Tip:** Because ceramic is thick, open the basket and give the eggs a quick stir with a fork at the 3-minute mark to help them cook evenly. Once fully set, carefully remove the hot dish using oven mitts.

### Step 3: The Grand Finale (Toast & Sizzle)
* **Settings:** 370°F for 1 to 2 minutes
* **Action:** Place your slices of bread directly onto the air fryer basket floor (or back in your empty silicone pan), and lay the pre-cooked sausages right next to them. 
* **Outcome:** The bread toasts incredibly fast! In just a minute or two, your bread will be perfectly crispy and your sausages will be re-heated and piping hot.

---

## Assembly
Stack your crispy toast, sizzling sausage, and fluffy scrambled egg patty together. Add cheese or condiments if desired, and enjoy!
