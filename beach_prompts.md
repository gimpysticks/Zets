### 1. The Classic Postcard (Short & Sweet)

> **A pristine white sand beach in Bora Bora, crystal clear turquoise water gently lapping the shore, leaning palm trees casting soft shadows. Bright midday sun creating a sparkling diamond effect on the water. Serene and idyllic paradise. Photorealistic, wide-angle shot, 8K. --ar 16:9**

### Pro-Tips for Your Prompting:

*   **Be Specific:** Instead of "South Pacific," try naming a specific place like **"Fiji," "Bora Bora," "Samoa,"** or **"the Cook Islands."** Midjourney has been trained on these locations and will pull specific visual cues.
*   **Time of Day is Key:** Use terms like **"midday sun," "golden hour," "sunrise,"** or **"blue hour"** to dramatically change the lighting and mood.
*   **Add a Focal Point:** Including an element like a **"thatched-roof hut," "hammock," "wooden boat,"** or **"overwater bungalow"** gives the AI something to focus on and makes the image more interesting.
*   **Camera & Style:** Words like **"photorealistic," "cinematic," "wide-angle lens," "drone shot,"** and **"National Geographic style"** are powerful commands that guide the final look.
*   **Aspect Ratio:** Use `--ar 16:9` for a widescreen, cinematic look, `--ar 3:2` for a standard photo look, or `--ar 9:16` for a vertical, phone wallpaper format.
