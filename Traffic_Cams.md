Here are the direct links to view live weather and traffic cameras in and around Northern Kentucky:

### **Road and Traffic Cameras (KYTC)**

The best source for real-time views of interstate and major road conditions in Northern Kentucky is the Kentucky Transportation Cabinet's (KYTC) traffic map, known as GoKY.

* **GoKY Traffic Cameras Map:** This interactive map allows you to zoom into the Northern Kentucky area (Boone, Kenton, and Campbell counties) and click on the **"Traffic Cam"** icon layer to see real-time snapshots of conditions on I-75, I-71, and I-275, including the Brent Spence Bridge area.
    * [GoKY Real-Time Traffic Map](https://goky.ky.gov/)

### **Weather and City View Webcams**

These cameras are typically maintained by local media or weather services and often provide a better view of general weather conditions.

* **Covington-Cincinnati Cam (Ohio River View):** This live streaming cam is located in Covington, KY, and offers a view across the Ohio River to the Cincinnati skyline, including the John A. Roebling Suspension Bridge, which is excellent for observing current weather and visibility.
    * [EarthCam: Cincinnati-Covington Cam](https://www.earthcam.com/usa/kentucky/covington/)
* **Melbourne, KY Weather Cam:** This is a listing for a webcam specifically located in Melbourne, Kentucky, which is in Campbell County.
    * [Melbourne, KY Live Weather Cameras & Webcams | WeatherBug](https://weather.weatherbug.com/weather-camera/melbourne-ky-41059)
* **Florence, KY Traffic Cameras:** This page provides a list and map of traffic camera views in the Florence area, which is in Boone County.
    * [Florence, KY Live Traffic Cameras & Local Road Conditions | WeatherBug](https://www.weatherbug.com/traffic-cam/florence-ky-41042)
* **Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky Airport (CVG) Area Webcams:** This page lists webcams near the Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky International Airport (CVG) for local wind and weather observation.
    * [Wind and weather webcams Cincinnati / Northern Kentucky Airport](https://www.windfinder.com/webcams/cincinnati_northern_kentucky_airport)
