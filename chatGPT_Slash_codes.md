Here is the clean list of slash commands and how to use them, with all image labels and call-to-action pages removed:

---

## How It Works

1. Type any of these codes before or after your prompt.
2. ChatGPT recognizes the command and shifts how it responds.
3. No setup. No settings. Just add the word.

---

## 1/ /steelman

Makes ChatGPT argue the strongest version of your idea instead of agreeing with it.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/steelman [your idea or argument]. Argue the strongest possible version of this. Best evidence, best logic, make it bulletproof. Then tell me the one weakness that still survives."

## 2/ /cheaper

Finds any product for less money before you buy it.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/cheaper [product name, link, or upload photo]. Find the same thing for less. Same quality, different brand. Show me where to buy it and the price difference."

## 3/ /rank

Rates every option and picks the single best one.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/rank [list options]. Compare all of these. Score each and tell me the best choice with reasoning. Don't hedge."

## 4/ /checklist

Turns any answer into steps you can follow and check off.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/checklist [your task]. Answer as a step-by-step checklist. Each step is one clear action. Nothing vague."

## 5/ /forecast

Maps the most likely outcomes before you decide.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/forecast [your decision]. Map 3 outcomes: best case, worst case, most probable. Be specific about what each looks like in 6 months."

## 6/ /risk

Finds every way it could go wrong before you commit.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/risk [your plan]. Find every way this fails. Rank by likelihood and impact. Tell me which risk I'm most likely to ignore."

## 7/ /timetravel

Shows you two versions of your future based on one decision.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/timetravel [your decision]. Write me two letters from myself 5 years from now. One where I did it, one where I didn't. Be specific about what my life looks like in each version."

## 8/ /gaps

Tells you the important questions you forgot to ask.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/gaps [your question]. Answer this, then tell me every follow-up I should have asked. Rank by how much they'd change my decision."

## 9/ /devil

Argues against you and pokes holes in everything.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/devil [your argument]. Take the opposing side. Find every flaw and weakness. Tell me which counterargument is strongest."

## 10/ /humanize

Makes any AI text sound like a real person wrote it.

Paste to ChatGPT:

> "/humanize [paste text]. Rewrite so it sounds human. Remove every phrase that sounds like AI. Keep my meaning, change the delivery."
