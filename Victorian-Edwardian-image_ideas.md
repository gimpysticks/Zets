**Victorian Era (c. 1837-1901) Prompt Ideas:**
1.  **Industrial Cityscape:** A bustling Victorian London street at dusk, gas lamps illuminating cobblestone, horse-drawn carriages, well-dressed pedestrians, and distant factory smoke stacks silhouetted against an orange sky.
2.  **Grand Ball Scene:** A lavish Victorian ballroom, couples waltzing in elaborate gowns and tailcoats, crystal chandeliers sparkling, ornate wallpaper, and a string quartet playing in the background.
3.  **Inventor's Workshop:** A cluttered, dimly lit workshop of a Victorian inventor, filled with gears, brass mechanisms, bubbling beakers, and schematic drawings, with an old man in a waistcoat intently focused on an intricate contraption.
4.  **Children at Play:** Victorian children in period attire playing with a wooden hoop and stick in a sunny, overgrown garden, a large country house in the background.
5.  **Seaside Resort:** A promenade at a Victorian seaside town, with elegantly dressed ladies holding parasols, gentlemen in straw boater hats, bathing machines on the beach, and a pier stretching into a calm sea.
6.  **Gothic Portrait:** A somber Victorian lady in a black lace dress, seated in a dimly lit parlor, holding a single wilting rose, with dark, heavy velvet drapes and an antique fireplace.
7.  **Steam Train Journey:** A powerful steam locomotive pulling passenger cars through a lush green countryside, a plume of smoke trailing, seen from a slightly elevated perspective, evoking a sense of adventure and travel.
8.  **Street Urchin:** A solitary Victorian street urchin with tattered clothes, warming hands over a brazier on a foggy London street, a looming brick tenement building behind them.
9.  **Early Photography Studio:** A Victorian-era photography studio, with a large wooden camera on a tripod, a subject posing stiffly in formal wear, and a photographer adjusting lighting, bathed in soft, diffused natural light from a skylight.
10. **Pre-Raphaelite Inspired Garden:** A beautiful young woman in a flowing, medieval-inspired Victorian gown, tending to a vibrant, wild rose garden, with a stone fountain and ivy-covered walls.
**Edwardian Era (c. 1901-1910, often extended to 1914) Prompt Ideas:**
1.  **Motorcar Outing:** An elegant Edwardian family in a stylish early automobile (like a Rolls-Royce Silver Ghost), driving through a scenic tree-lined country lane, picnic basket visible, sunshine dappling through leaves.
2.  **Suffragette March:** A line of determined Edwardian women in white dresses and sashes, holding "Votes for Women" banners, marching down a bustling city street, a crowd of onlookers in the background.
3.  **Garden Party:** A vibrant Edwardian garden party scene, ladies in large hats and lighter dresses, gentlemen in tailored suits, enjoying tea and croquet on a manicured lawn of a grand country estate, bright daylight.
4.  **Luxury Liner Deck:** Passengers in elegant Edwardian attire strolling on the deck of a grand ocean liner (e.g., Titanic-era ship), looking out at the vast, calm sea, with ornate railings and lifeboats visible.
5.  **First Flight:** A daring early aviator in a leather helmet and goggles, standing proudly next to a biplane on a grassy field, onlookers gathering in awe, clear blue sky overhead.
6.  **Telegraph Office:** An Edwardian telegraph office, operators busily sending messages, flickering electric lights, intricate machinery, a sense of quiet technological advancement.
7.  **Arts and Crafts Interior:** A cozy Edwardian living room designed in the Arts and Crafts style, with dark wood furniture, stained glass windows, a large fireplace, and a family reading or playing board games.
8.  **Tennis Match:** An energetic Edwardian tennis match in progress on a grass court, players in traditional white attire, spectators gathered under striped awnings, late afternoon sun.
9.  **Music Hall Performance:** A vibrant Edwardian music hall stage, a vaudeville performer in an elaborate costume singing under bright stage lights, an audience laughing and clapping in the shadowy foreground.
10. **Picnic in the Park:** An Edwardian couple sharing a romantic picnic in a beautifully landscaped park, with a wicker basket, blanket, and scenic trees, capturing a moment of serene leisure.
