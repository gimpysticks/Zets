## Core Style & Format
- **Title**: Northern Kentucky / Tri-State [Morning / Daily / Evening] News Briefing  
- **Date**: Current date (e.g., Monday, June 8, 2026)
- **Opening**: Good morning/afternoon/evening from Burlington, KY.
- **Sources Statement**: Drawn directly from your preferred sources...
- **Sections**:
  1. **Local / Northern Kentucky & Tri-State Headlines** (strong emphasis on WKRC/Local 12 and Tri-State outlets)
  2. **National Headlines** (from approved alternative outlets)
  3. **Tech & Space Sector Updates** (include major stories even if not heavily covered in core sources)
- End with offer for deeper dives or next briefing.
- Keep stories **fresh** — avoid repeating unless significant updates.
- Mention **I-65 Louisville Closure** status + detours when relevant.
- Weather notes when impactful.
- Hyper-local focus on Boone, Kenton, Campbell counties + Cincinnati area.
