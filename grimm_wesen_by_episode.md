# Grimm – Wesen by Episode

**Scope:** Each episode includes a concise story synopsis and the **primary featured Wesen** (major antagonists or story-driving creatures). Background or incidental Wesen are not exhaustively listed.

---

## Season 1 (2011–2012)

1. **Pilot** **Wesen:** [`Blutbad`](grimm_wesen.md#blutbad)  
   **Synopsis:** Portland detective Nick Burkhardt discovers he is a Grimm when he begins seeing monstrous faces and investigates a series of kidnappings linked to a wolf-like Wesen.

2. **Bears Will Be Bears** **Wesen:** [`Jägerbar`](grimm_wesen.md#jägerbar)  
   **Synopsis:** A killing at a gym leads Nick to a bear-like Wesen struggling to suppress violent instincts while adapting to modern life.

3. **BeeWare** **Wesen:** [`Mellifer`](grimm_wesen.md#mellifer)  
   **Synopsis:** A robbery tied to a honey-based drug exposes a hive-like Wesen whose venom creates powerful addiction.

4. **Lonelyhearts** **Wesen:** [`Seelengut`](grimm_wesen.md#seelengut)  
   **Synopsis:** Online dating murders are traced to a Wesen that steals human organs to maintain beauty and youth.

5. **Danse Macabre** **Wesen:** [`Ziegevolk`](grimm_wesen.md#ziegevolk)  
   **Synopsis:** A high school outbreak of violent behavior reveals a Wesen whose presence triggers uncontrollable rage in others.

6. **The Thing with Feathers** **Wesen:** [`Bauerschwein`](grimm_wesen.md#bauerschwein)  
   **Synopsis:** An abusive farm family hides a dark secret connected to pig-like Wesen and a cycle of vengeance.

7. **Let Your Hair Down** **Wesen:** [`Fuchsbau`](grimm_wesen.md#fuchsbau)  
   **Synopsis:** A feral girl raised in isolation challenges Nick’s understanding of Wesen morality and survival.

8. **Game Ogre** **Wesen:** Ogre  
   **Synopsis:** A brutal underground boxing ring is run by a monstrous Wesen who feeds on violence and domination.

9. **Of Mouse and Man** **Wesen:** Mauzhertz  
   **Synopsis:** A bullied teenager with rodent-like traits is driven to revenge after years of abuse.

10. **Organ Grinder** **Wesen:** [`Aswang`](grimm_wesen.md#aswang)  
   **Synopsis:** A string of child abductions leads to a creature harvesting organs for profit.

11. **Tarantella** **Wesen:** [`Spinnetod`](grimm_wesen.md#spinnetod)  
   **Synopsis:** A seductive dance instructor hides a deadly spider-like nature tied to ritualized killings.

12. **Last Grimm Standing** **Wesen:** [`Blutbad`](grimm_wesen.md#blutbad)  
   **Synopsis:** Nick infiltrates a secret Wesen fight club where combat determines survival and status.

13. **Three Coins in a Fuchsbau** **Wesen:** [`Verrat`](grimm_wesen.md#verrat) (assassins)  
   **Synopsis:** Ancient Grimm coins spark international conflict as Nick learns the deadly cost of Grimm artifacts.

14. **Plumed Serpent** **Wesen:** Feathered Serpent Wesen  
   **Synopsis:** A charismatic cult leader sacrifices followers under the belief he is a living god.

15. **Island of Dreams** **Wesen:** [`Seltenvogel`](grimm_wesen.md#seltenvogel)  
   **Synopsis:** A rare and beautiful Wesen becomes the target of obsession and murder.

16. **Love Sick** **Wesen:** Cupiditas  
   **Synopsis:** A love-inducing Wesen spreads chaos as Nick struggles with uncontrollable attraction.

17. **Cat and Mouse** **Wesen:** Mauzhertz  
   **Synopsis:** Nick protects a frightened Wesen witness hunted by her own kind.

18. **Leave It to Beavers** **Wesen:** [`Bieber`](grimm_wesen.md#eisbiber)  
   **Synopsis:** Suburban perfection hides a family of beaver-like Wesen with violent traditions.

19. **Happily Ever Aftermath** **Wesen:** [`Blutbad`](grimm_wesen.md#blutbad)  
   **Synopsis:** A marriage counselor uncovers lethal secrets when helping a troubled Wesen couple.

20. **Big Feet** **Wesen:** Siebengeist  
   **Synopsis:** A reclusive Wesen inspires fear and legend after years of persecution.

21. **Woman in Black** **Wesen:** [`Hexenbiest`](grimm_wesen.md#hexenbiest--zauberbiest)  
   **Synopsis:** Adalind returns, forcing Nick to confront powerful royal forces and dark magic.
