
**Here are some excellent beginner videos for learning how to drive a Tesla.** These cover basics like getting in, starting/driving, regenerative braking, controls, charging, and more. I’ve included a mix of official Tesla content and popular third-party guides.

### Official Tesla Resources (Highly Recommended)
- **Tesla Support Video Guides**: Official short videos on driving, controls, regenerative braking, and more (filter by "Driving").  
  https://www.tesla.com/support/videos

- **Meet Your Tesla Series** (Model 3 / Model Y): Official fundamentals including access, driver controls, storage, and regenerative braking.  
  - Model 3: https://www.tesla.com/support/meet-your-tesla/model-3  
  - Model Y: https://www.tesla.com/support/meet-your-tesla/model-y

- **Full Self-Driving (Supervised) Official Tutorial** (from Tesla Tutorials): Great intro if your Tesla has it.  
  https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1mFz3PYTwx8

**Quick tips before watching**: Teslas have no traditional ignition— just get in (with phone key or card), press the brake, and shift via the touchscreen or stalk (depending on model year). They use one-pedal driving with strong regenerative braking. Always stay attentive, especially with Autopilot features. Check your specific model’s owner’s manual in the car’s touchscreen for tailored info.
