
Here's a list of notable artists who worked for Mad Magazine:

* Paul Peter Porges - Regular contributor from 1971, known for energetic line work and monochrome wash
* Scott Bricher
* Tom Bunk
* Bob Clarke
* Paul Coker
* Jack Davis - Early regular artist
* Mort Drucker
* Will Elder - Early regular artist
* Hermann Mejia
* Joe Orlando
* Tom Richmond
* Jack Rickard - Over 175 issues, 35 covers
* John Severin - Early contributor
* Angelo Torres
* Rick Tulka
* Sam Viviano
* Wally Wood - Early regular artist
* George Woodbridge
* Dave Berg - Known for 'The Lighter Side' feature
* Basil Wolverton - Early contributor

These artists helped create Mad's signature style of pop-culture parodies and social satire over its long run.
