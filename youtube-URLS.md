*   **For Ad-Free Viewing:** Add a **hyphen (-)** between the **"t"** and the **"u"** in "youtube.com".

*   **For Downloading (Full HD, MP4, or MP3):** Remove the letters **"u," "b," and "e"** from the word "tube" in the URL.
    *   *Example Change:* `youtube.com/...` becomes `yout.com/...`
