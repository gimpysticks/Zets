# 21 ChatGPT Prompts To Learn ANYTHING

---

## Find My Starting Point
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a tutor, ask me 10 questions to figure out what I already know about [topic], then tell me where I should begin"

---

## Build A Learning Plan
**Prompt:**  
"Act as an expert teacher; I want to learn [topic] from scratch, map out the key stages in the right order so I know what to learn first, next, and later"

---

## Explain It Simply
**Prompt:**  
"Act like a great middle school teacher, explain [topic] like I'm 12, use plain English, short examples, and no jargon"

---

## Break It Down
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a curriculum designer, split [topic] into small parts I can study one at a time, and explain why each part matters"

---

## Make A Study Plan
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a study coach, create a 30 day plan to learn [topic] in short daily sessions, keep it realistic and beginner friendly"

---

## Teach Me Step By Step
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a patient tutor, teach me [topic] one step at a time, do not move on until I show I understand the last step"

---

## Give Me The Basics
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a great teacher, show me the 20 percent of ideas in [topic] that will help me understand 80 percent of it"

---

## Define Key Terms
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a beginner friendly instructor, list the most important words in [topic], define each one simply, and give an easy example for each"

---

## Compare Concepts
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a clear thinking teacher, compare [concept A] and [concept B] in simple terms, and show where people get confused"

---

## Use Analogies
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a creative teacher, teach me [topic] using everyday analogies so the ideas are easier to remember"

---

## Quiz Me
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a quiz coach, quiz me on [topic] with one question at a time, wait for my answer, then explain what I got right or wrong"

---

## Turn Notes Into Lessons
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a note taking expert, here's what I wrote about [topic], clean it up, fill in gaps, and turn it into simple study notes"

---

## Make Flashcards
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a memory coach, turn this lesson on [topic] into flashcards with short questions and clear answers"

---

## Create Practice Drills
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a skills trainer, give me 10 practice exercises for [topic], start easy and get harder as I improve"

---

## Show Real Examples
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a practical teacher, give me 5 real world examples of [topic] so I can see how it actually works"

---

## Fix My Mistakes
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a supportive tutor, look at my answer or work on [topic], point out mistakes, explain why they happened, and show the better way"

---

## Create A Cheat Sheet
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a study guide expert, turn [topic] into a one page cheat sheet with the key ideas, rules, steps, and common mistakes"

---

## Test My Weak Spots
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a learning assessor, give me a short diagnostic quiz on [topic], then show me my weak areas and what to study next"

---

## Make It Memorable
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a memory expert, help me remember [topic] using memory tricks, patterns, and simple ways to recall it fast"

---

## Connect The Dots
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a big picture teacher, show me how the parts of [topic] connect, and how one idea leads into the next"

---

## Apply It To Real Life
**Prompt:**  
"Act as a coach, show me how to use [topic] in real life, give me 5 practical situations where I'd apply it"
