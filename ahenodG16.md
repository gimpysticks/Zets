Factory Reset for AHENOD G16 Music Player

To perform a factory reset on the AHENOD G16 music player, follow these steps:
Method 1: Using the Settings Menu

    Power On: Turn on your AHENOD G16.

    Access Settings: Navigate to the "Settings" menu from the home screen.

    Find Reset Option: Look for an option labeled "Backup & Reset" or "System."

    Select Factory Data Reset: Choose "Factory Data Reset" and confirm your selection.

    Confirm Reset: Follow the prompts to confirm the reset. This will erase all data and restore the device to its original settings.

Method 2: Using Hardware Buttons

    Power Off: Ensure the device is completely powered off.

    Press and Hold Buttons: Simultaneously press and hold the "Power" button and the "Volume Up" button.

    Enter Recovery Mode: Release the buttons when the AHENOD logo appears.

    Navigate Recovery Menu: Use the volume buttons to navigate to "Wipe Data/Factory Reset" and select it using the power button.

    Confirm Reset: Choose "Yes" to confirm the factory reset.

Important Notes

    Data Loss: A factory reset will erase all personal data, including music files and settings.

    Backup: Consider backing up important files before proceeding.

    Charging: Ensure the device is charged or connected to power during the reset process to avoid interruptions.

Following these steps will successfully reset your AHENOD G16 music player to factory settings.
