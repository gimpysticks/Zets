 INSPIRATION & VISUAL IDEAS

 Who or What to Depict:

· The Hunt Leader: Odin on an eight-legged horse, a horned figure, a woman in black armor with a crow mask.
· The Hounds: Massive black dogs with glowing red or amber eyes, baying at the moon.
· The Riders: Ghostly warriors on horseback, translucent or wreathed in lightning, carrying spears and torches.
· The Hunted: A lone traveler or animal fleeing through misty woods, looking back in terror.
· The Aftermath: Abandoned tracks, broken branches, hoofprints that glow faintly in the morning dew.

 Style Inspirations:

· Dark fantasy illustration – think The Witcher meets Celtic myth
· Atmospheric photography – misty forests, storm clouds, torchlight
· Surreal digital painting – blending ghostly figures with real landscapes
· Animated loops – riders moving across the sky, hounds running endlessly
· Cinematic stills – high contrast, dramatic shadows, lightning flashes

 Palette:

· Stormy greys, deep indigo, charcoal black
· Accents: pale spectral blue, ghostly white, blood red (eyes, torches, or cloaks)
· Occasional gold or amber for a leader's crown or hounds' eyes

 For Video (Reels):

· Slow-motion riders galloping through fog
· A single hound's eye glowing, then a pack emerging
· Trees bending in an unseen wind, hoofbeats growing louder
· A lightning strike revealing a spectral army for one frame
